2. Technical Hosting (GitHub Pages)

This website is hosted externally via GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc., 88 Colin P Kelly Jr St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA.

If you contact us via our provided e-mail address, your e-mail, its metadata, and the personal data contained within it will be stored by us for the sole purpose of processing your inquiry. We do not pass this data on without your consent. The legal basis for processing this data is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual relations) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

5. Your Rights as a User

Under the GDPR, you have the right to request information about your stored personal data, its origin, recipients, and the purpose of its collection at no charge. You also have the right to request that it be corrected, blocked, or deleted. To exercise these rights, simply email us.

Furthermore, you have the right to lodge a formal complaint with the competent supervisory authority. For our studio, this is the State Commissioner for Data Protection of Baden-Württemberg (LfDI Baden-Württemberg).
